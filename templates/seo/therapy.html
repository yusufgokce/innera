Therapy That Meets You Where You Are

At Innera, we believe therapy is not one-size-fits-all. Elif Gökçe integrates multiple evidence-based therapeutic approaches to create a personalized experience that honors your unique needs, cultural background, and goals. Whether you're navigating anxiety, trauma, relationship challenges, or life transitions, our integrative approach provides the flexibility and depth needed for meaningful change.

With over 20 years of experience in counseling and education, and advanced training from Emmanuel College at the University of Toronto, Elif brings compassion, cultural sensitivity, and expertise to every session. All therapy is conducted virtually via secure, PIPEDA-certified Google Meet, making quality care accessible throughout the Greater Toronto Area.

Our Therapeutic Approaches

Each approach below is grounded in research and tailored to your specific needs. Click any therapy type to learn more about how it works and what it can help with.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

An evidence-based approach that helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. Effective for anxiety, depression, and stress management.

Learn More →

Trauma-Informed Approach

Recognizes the widespread impact of trauma and emphasizes safety, trustworthiness, and empowerment in the healing process. Ideal for PTSD, complex trauma, and religious trauma.

Learn More →

Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)

Combines cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness practices. Highly effective for emotion regulation, relationship challenges, anger management, and ADHD.

Learn More →

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Integrates present-moment awareness with psychotherapy to reduce stress, anxiety, and chronic pain. Cultivates self-compassion and emotional balance.

Learn More →

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Focuses on accepting difficult emotions while committing to values-based action. Effective for anxiety, depression, and chronic pain.

Learn More →

Relational/Attachment Therapy

Explores how early relationships shape current patterns. Helps heal attachment wounds and build healthier connections.

Learn More →

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy

A goal-oriented approach that focuses on solutions rather than problems. Ideal for clients seeking practical, time-limited therapy.

Learn More →

Narrative Therapy

Views you as separate from your problems, empowering you to rewrite your life story. Particularly helpful for identity exploration and meaning-making.

Learn More →

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

Explores the different "parts" within you, helping integrate inner conflicts and access your core self. Effective for trauma, anxiety, and self-criticism.

Learn More →

Person-Centred (Rogerian) Approach

Creates a warm, non-judgmental space where you lead the conversation. Emphasizes empathy, genuineness, and unconditional positive regard.

Learn More →

Spiritual/Pastoral Care

Integrates spirituality and faith into the therapeutic process. Addresses existential questions, religious trauma, grief, and meaning-making.

Learn More →
