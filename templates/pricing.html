We provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement. Most extended health plans in Canada cover psychotherapy services.

Payment is due at the time of service, and we'll provide an official receipt you can use to claim from your insurance.

Common Insurance Providers

Most major Canadian insurance companies cover psychotherapy services, including: Manulife, Sun Life Financial, Canada Life, Great-West Life, Blue Cross, Desjardins, Industrial Alliance, Equitable Life, and many others.

Please verify your specific coverage with your insurance provider before booking. Look for coverage under "Registered Psychotherapist" or "Mental Health Practitioner."
